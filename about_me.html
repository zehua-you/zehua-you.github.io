Current Self

Hi, welcome to my personal website again!! My name is Zehua You. I am a current student at Swarthmore College, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Sociology.

I have a lot of interests and hobbies. I like reading books, especially literature and philosophy texts. Literature allows me to get lost in stories and stepping into the world that I will never have the chance to explore in my life. Philosophy invites me to ponder deeply and understand why I am doing something before I do it.

I also enjoy music and singing when I am free because I think music helps me connect the gap between logic and emotions.
